## Post (copy below the line)

---

most drug discovery tools answer "is this molecule good?" — wrong question.

the right question is "which molecules are non-dominated across *all* the properties that matter?" that's a Pareto problem, and nobody gives you a scatter plot for it.

so i built MolParetoLab. paste SMILES, get instant multi-objective Pareto analysis — MW, cLogP, HBD, HBA, TPSA, rotatable bonds. interactive scatter plots with configurable axes. BOILED-Egg BBB/GI prediction. dominance matrices. side-by-side molecule comparison. four drug-likeness filters (Ro5, Veber, Ghose, lead-like) as toggle overlays.

the whole thing runs client-side via RDKit WASM. no server, no installs, no data leaves your browser. it's a single HTML file.

100 compounds enter preclinical. 7 make it to approval. if you're not triaging on multi-objective trade-offs before the first assay, you're optimizing in the dark.

try it: https://molparetolab.ilkham.com

open source: https://github.com/IlkhamFY/molparetolab
